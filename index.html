About Us

Sagar Dance Studio

"Sagar Dance Studio is a premier dance studio located in Vadodara, Gujarat. We offer a variety of dance classes for all ages and levels, including hip-hop, bollywood, freestyle, and more. Our experienced and passionate instructors will help you reach your full potential as a dancer. We believe that dance is more than just a hobby. It is a way to express yourself, build confidence, and have fun. We offer a variety of classes to fit your schedule, including day, evening, and weekend classes. We also offer private lessons for those who want more individualized attention. We offer a variety of classes for all ages and levels, and our experienced instructors will help you reach your full potential."
